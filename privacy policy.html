Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Pagge ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our AI Paraphrasing Tool, available at pagaetool.com (or your deployed domain).

1. Information We Collect

PagAE is designed to prioritize your privacy. We do not collect or store personal data unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., through the contact form). The following information may be collected only under specific conditions:

  • Text Input: Any text you upload or enter is used only for processing via the Gemini API and is not stored.
  • Contact Form Data: If you contact us, we may collect your name, email, and message content solely for communication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide only for the following purposes:

  • To process and paraphrase input text using Gemini AI.
  • To respond to your messages, support requests, or feedback.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of Page.

3. Use of Gemini API

PagAE uses Google Gemini API to perform AI-based paraphrasing. Input text is temporarily sent to the Gemini model for processing. We do not save or reuse any data submitted for paraphrasing. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Gemini may handle the data during processing.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any uploaded or entered text after the paraphrasing operation is complete. Contact messages are retained only as long as needed to respond or resolve your request.

5. Cookies & Tracking

PagAE does not use cookies or tracking technologies for analytics, advertising, or behavioral profiling. Basic performance logs may be used for site maintenance purposes only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your contact data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (if applicable)

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we advise caution when sharing sensitive content online.
